Experience during MCO period

I will show you my activity that i have done during the MCO due to the spread of the Covid-19 virus.

New Hobby in MCO

During the durations of this movement control order, I really love to cook even though I am not very good at it. Cooking is a skill for survival because it teaches me to be independent, and it's a good way to save money instead of eating out. Not only is it cheaper to eat at home, but when you cook at home, you know exactly the type and quality of ingredients that go into the meal and you'll be sure that you're eating only wholesome, nutritious food.

Charity For Residents During The MCO Period

Amidst social distancing, financial uncertainty and economic downturn, i joined the team NGO at Sungai Petani, Kedah to provide assistance to famalies affected by the Covid-19 virus. This campaign takes into account struggling famalies whoes head of household has suffered pay cuts, loss of a job, had their children laid off, or other implications that have been brought on by the outbreak.
